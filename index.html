Our Story

The range offered by us includes Spices Grinding Plants, Macro Pulverizers,Mixers,to name a few. These machines & process equipments are manufactured as per the international standards and norms using superior quality raw materials. Owing to their features such as high performance , low maintenance and low cost,these machines are widely demanded by the clients. Pulverizer

Our offered Pulverizer are available in various sizes and dimensions and are getting high appreciations for the specific features such as longer service life, optimum performance,cost effective and robust construction.A multipurpose pulverizing machine works on the principle of powdering materials due to impact in-between swing hammers and fixed serrated plates. Material enters the impact pulverizer through feeding tray to pulverizing chamber and gets powdered. The finely powdered material will be carried away with air pressure through the blower to the cyclone separator, where product can be collected.
